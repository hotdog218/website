Trouble in Terrorist Town

General Rules

 

1.   No KOS ( Kill-On-Sight ) on location. Even if you hear/notice a gunshot come out of

a room for instance, go into that room, and only one person is there, you *cannot* KOS

them! You *can* place heavy suspicion, saying they're all but confirmed T.

2.   No KOS ( Kill-On-Sight ) on skins or trails! If someone is using a disguiser, and you

call them out, if they say they were using disguiser and I see this with console, you

*will* be slain for KOS on skin!

3.   You may give another player 3 warnings in sequential order to cease following

you. If, after the third warning, they persist, you may use lethal force against them,

even if they are innocent, you will not be slain.

4.   No RDM ( Random Deathmatch ). This means you can't shoot people for no

reason, dammit!

5.   Detectives may claim rooms with their spray. After spraying, and calling it out in

chat, anyone who walks past, knowingly or not, may be shot."

6.   Detectives may order players to use the Traitor Tester, and can shoot them if

they do not comply. ' Proven ' players *CAN NOT FORCE YOU TO USE THE TESTER*."

7.   Prop Blocking is allowed.

8.   Attempting to hit someone with a prop ( At lethal speeds, not slight bumps ) is

considered KOS-able.

9.   Innocents may claim rooms after Overtime has started.

Map Specific Rules

1.   Turning off the lights on Clue is KOS-able.

2.   Approaching an individual with a mine on Whitehouse is KOS-able.

3.   Discombobulators are KOS-able on Metropolis, Teen Room, Rooftops, and any

other high area maps with lethal falls. They are NOT KOS-able at ground level on maps

like Teen Room.
